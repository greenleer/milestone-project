About Me

Hi, I'm Ryan!

I'm a self-taught Front-end Web Developer and Designer, living in Denver, CO.

I'm also a music composer, amateur photographer, and snowboarder. My journey with web development started when I couldn't find a Wordpress theme that I liked. Driven by curiosity, I enrolled in the Break Into Tech cirriculum at Skillcrush, and I haven't looked back.

When I'm not designing websites, you can find me hiking through the Rocky Mountains of Colorado, or playing with my nephews. They think I'm super cool.
